# IELTS 30-Day Plan (3.0 → 5.0)

**Exam date:** July 20, 2026  
**Daily study time:** 3 hours  
**Focus:** IELTS Academic

## Weekly Overview

| Week | Dates | Focus |
|------|-------|-------|
| Week 1 | 20/6 – 26/6 | Foundation: grammar, basic vocab, test format |
| Week 2 | 27/6 – 3/7 | Skill building: question types, Writing/Speaking structures |
| Week 3 | 4/7 – 10/7 | Intensive practice: full sections, timed work |
| Week 4 | 11/7 – 17/7 | Mock tests + weak-area polish |
| Final | 18/7 – 19/7 | Light review + rest |

## Daily Schedule (3 hours)

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 45–60 min | Listening |
| 45–60 min | Reading |
| 30–45 min | Writing OR Speaking (alternate) |
| 15–30 min | Vocabulary / Grammar review |

## Week 1: Foundation

| Day | Date | Main Tasks                                                                                               |
| --- | ---- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | 20/6 | **Diagnostic test** full (Cambridge IELTS 10 Test 1). Score and identify weakest skill.                  |
| 2   | 21/6 | Grammar: present simple, present continuous, past simple. Vocab: Education (20 words). Listening Part 1. |
| 3   | 22/6 | Grammar: present perfect, future forms. Vocab: Work (20 words). Reading question types overview.         |
| 4   | 23/6 | Grammar: articles, plurals. Vocab: Family (20 words). Listening Part 1 practice (2 tests).               |
| 5   | 24/6 | Grammar: prepositions of time/place. Vocab: Travel (20 words). Reading gap-fill practice.                |
| 6   | 25/6 | Listening Part 2 (maps, matching). Reading True/False/Not Given. Review 100 words from Week 1.           |
| 7   | 26/6 | Light review. 1 Listening Part 1 + 1 short Reading passage. Rest.                                        |

## Week 2: Skill Building

| Day | Date | Main Tasks |
|-----|------|------------|
| 8 | 27/6 | Listening: 2 Part 1 + 1 Part 2. Grammar: first conditional. |
| 9 | 28/6 | Reading: skim/scan. 1 full Passage 1 (Cambridge 11). Vocab: Environment. |
| 10 | 29/6 | Writing Task 1 Academic: structure + overview. Practice line/bar chart. |
| 11 | 30/6 | Writing Task 2: 4-paragraph structure. Write introduction + 1 body paragraph. |
| 12 | 1/7 | Speaking Part 1: 20 common questions. Record yourself. Vocab: Hometown/Study. |
| 13 | 2/7 | Listening Part 3 (multiple choice + matching). Grammar: relative clauses. |
| 14 | 3/7 | Mini test: 1 Listening full + 1 Reading full. Review errors. |

## Week 3: Intensive Practice

| Day | Date | Main Tasks                                                           |
| --- | ---- | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 15  | 4/7  | Reading full test (60 min). Analyze wrong answers. Vocab: Health.    |
| 16  | 5/7  | Listening full test (30 min) + transcript review. Vocab: Technology. |
| 17  | 6/7  | Writing: 1 Task 1 (20 min) + 1 Task 2 (40 min). Self-check.          |
| 18  | 7/7  | Speaking Part 2: 5 cue cards. Record 2-minute answers.               |
| 19  | 8/7  | Listening Part 3–4 + Reading Passage 2–3. Vocab review.              |
| 20  | 9/7  | **Full mock test 1** (Cambridge 11 Test 2, timed). Score.            |
| 21  | 10/7 | Rest + review mock test mistakes.                                    |

## Week 4: Mock Tests + Polish

| Day | Date | Main Tasks |
|-----|------|------------|
| 22 | 11/7 | Focus on weakest skill from mock test 1. Extra drills. |
| 23 | 12/7 | Writing focus: Task 1 table/pie/process + Task 2 common topics. |
| 24 | 13/7 | Speaking focus: full mock speaking test + Part 2 timing. |
| 25 | 14/7 | **Full mock test 2** (Cambridge 12 Test 1, timed). Score. |
| 26 | 15/7 | Review mock 2. Grammar: passive voice, comparatives. Synonyms. |
| 27 | 16/7 | **Full mock test 3** (Cambridge 12 Test 2, timed). Final score check. |
| 28 | 17/7 | Final review: Writing templates, Speaking cue cards, high-frequency vocab. |

## Final Days

| Day | Date | Main Tasks |
|-----|------|------------|
| 29 | 18/7 | Light review only. Prepare ID/documents. Sleep early. |
| 30 | 19/7 | **Rest day.** No heavy study. Mental preparation. |
| Exam | 20/7 | **IELTS Academic exam day.** |
