# Daily 3-Hour Study Schedule

## Option A: Balanced (default)

| Block | Time | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| 1 | 45–60 min | **Listening** — 1 part or 1 full section |
| 2 | 45–60 min | **Reading** — 1 passage or 1 section |
| 3 | 30–45 min | **Writing OR Speaking** — alternate daily |
| 4 | 15–30 min | **Vocabulary / Grammar review** |

## Option B: Writing-Heavy Day

| Block | Time | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| 1 | 45 min | Listening Part 1–2 |
| 2 | 45 min | Reading 1 passage |
| 3 | 60 min | Writing Task 1 + Task 2 |
| 4 | 30 min | Vocabulary review |

## Option C: Speaking-Heavy Day

| Block | Time | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| 1 | 45 min | Listening Part 3–4 |
| 2 | 45 min | Reading 1 passage |
| 3 | 60 min | Speaking Part 1/2/3 practice + recording |
| 4 | 30 min | Grammar review |

## Weekly Rotation Suggestion

| Day | Writing/Speaking Focus |
|-----|------------------------|
| Mon | Writing Task 1 |
| Tue | Writing Task 2 |
| Wed | Speaking Part 1 |
| Thu | Speaking Part 2 |
| Fri | Writing Task 1 + 2 |
| Sat | Speaking full mock |
| Sun | Review + light practice |

## Tips

- Use a timer. Stop when the block ends.
- Do NOT multitask (no phone, no social media).
- Take 5-minute breaks between blocks.
- At the end of each day, run `/ielts-study-tracker`.
