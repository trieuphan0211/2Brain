# Problem-Solution Essays

## Question Type

The question asks: **What are the problems and what solutions can you suggest?**

## Structure

1. Introduction — introduce the problem.
2. Body 1 — problem 1 + explanation.
3. Body 2 — solution 1 + solution 2.
4. Conclusion — summarize problems and solutions.

## Useful Phrases

- One major problem is ...
- This is because ...
- A possible solution is ...
- The government should ...
- In conclusion, ...

## Sample Question

> In many cities, traffic congestion is a serious problem. What are the causes of this and what measures can be taken to solve it?
