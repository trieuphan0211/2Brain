# Band 5.0 Sample Essays

## Sample 1: Opinion Essay

**Question:** Some people think that children should start school as early as possible. Do you agree or disagree?

**Essay:**

> In my opinion, I agree that children should start school early. This is because early education helps children develop social skills and learn basic knowledge.
>
> First, going to school early helps children make friends. When children meet other children every day, they learn how to talk and play together. For example, my nephew started school at four years old, and now he has many friends. He is also more confident than before.
>
> Second, starting school early gives children basic knowledge. They can learn numbers, letters, and simple words. This is important for their future study. If children learn these things early, they will have a better start in primary school.
>
> In conclusion, I believe children should start school early because it improves their social skills and gives them basic knowledge. Therefore, parents should consider sending their children to school at a young age.

## Why This Is Band 5.0

- Clear opinion throughout.
- Simple but mostly correct grammar.
- Basic linking words.
- Ideas are relevant but not deeply developed.
- Vocabulary is adequate but repetitive.
