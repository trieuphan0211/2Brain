# Opinion Essays

## Question Type

The question asks: **Do you agree or disagree?** or **What is your opinion?**

## Structure

1. Introduction — state your opinion clearly.
2. Body 1 — reason 1 + example.
3. Body 2 — reason 2 + example.
4. Conclusion — restate opinion.

## Useful Phrases

- I strongly agree that ...
- In my opinion, ...
- I believe this because ...
- For example, ...
- Therefore, ...

## Sample Question

> Some people think that the best way to reduce crime is to give criminals longer prison sentences. To what extent do you agree or disagree?
