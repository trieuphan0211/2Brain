# Discussion Essays

## Question Type

The question asks: **Discuss both views and give your opinion.**

## Structure

1. Introduction — introduce both views + your opinion.
2. Body 1 — first view + example.
3. Body 2 — second view + example.
4. Conclusion — restate your opinion.

## Useful Phrases

- On the one hand, ...
- On the other hand, ...
- Some people argue that ...
- However, others believe that ...
- In my view, ...

## Sample Question

> Some people think that the internet has brought people closer together, while others believe it has pushed them apart. Discuss both views and give your opinion.
