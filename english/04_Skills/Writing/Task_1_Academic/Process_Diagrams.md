# Process Diagrams

## What to Describe

- Stages of the process
- Inputs and outputs
- Sequence (first, then, next, finally)
- Any changes or transformations

## Useful Phrases

- The process begins with ...
- In the next stage, ...
- Following this, ...
- The final product is ...
- At the same time, ...
