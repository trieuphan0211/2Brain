# Line Graphs

## What to Describe

- Overall trend (increase/decrease/stable)
- Peaks and lows
- Comparisons between lines
- Significant changes

## Useful Phrases

- The figure for X rose from ... to ...
- There was a sharp increase in ...
- X reached a peak of ... in ...
- X remained stable at around ...
- By contrast, Y fell steadily.
