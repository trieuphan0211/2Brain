# Bar Charts

## What to Describe

- Highest and lowest bars
- Differences between categories
- Trends over time (if grouped)
- Comparisons

## Useful Phrases

- X had the highest figure at ...
- Y was significantly lower than X.
- The most popular choice was ...
- In contrast, the least popular was ...
