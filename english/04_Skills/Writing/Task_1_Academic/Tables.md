# Tables

## What to Describe

- Highest and lowest figures
- Patterns across rows or columns
- Significant differences
- Rankings

## Useful Phrases

- The highest figure was recorded in ...
- The lowest percentage was in ...
- X was ranked first, followed by Y.
- There was a noticeable difference between ...
