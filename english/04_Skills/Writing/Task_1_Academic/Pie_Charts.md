# Pie Charts

## What to Describe

- Largest and smallest segments
- Percentages
- Comparisons between pie charts
- Changes over time

## Useful Phrases

- X accounted for the largest proportion at ...%.
- Y made up ...% of the total.
- The smallest segment was ..., at ...%.
- By comparison, Z represented ...%.
