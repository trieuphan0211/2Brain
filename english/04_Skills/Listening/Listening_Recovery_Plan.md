# Listening Recovery Plan (5/40 → 16/40)

**Current score:** 5/40 (~Band 2.5–3.0)  
**Target score:** 16/40 (~Band 5.0)  
**Time:** 30 days

## Why the Score Is Low

At 5/40, you likely struggle with:
- Speed of speech
- Not recognizing common words
- Spelling mistakes
- Losing focus during the recording
- Not reading questions fast enough

## Strategy

### Week 1: Part 1 Only

- Target: **6–7/10** in Part 1
- Practice: 1–2 Part 1 tests per day
- Focus: names, numbers, dates, addresses, spelling

### Week 2: Part 1 + Part 2

- Target: **5–6/10** in Part 2
- Practice: 1 Part 1 + 1 Part 2 per day
- Focus: maps, matching, multiple choice

### Week 3: Part 1 + 2 + 3

- Target: **4–5/10** in Part 3
- Practice: 1 full Listening section per day
- Focus: following multiple speakers

### Week 4: Full Sections + Mock Tests

- Target: stable **16+/40**
- Practice: full Listening tests under timed conditions

## Daily Listening Routine (60–90 min)

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 20 min | IELTS Listening practice (one Part or section) |
| 15 min | Listen again with transcript, mark unknown words |
| 15 min | Dictation: write what you hear from short clips |
| 15 min | Graded listening (BBC Learning English, ESL Lab) |
| 15 min | Review vocabulary from transcripts |

## Recommended Graded Listening

- **BBC Learning English** (6 Minute English)
- **Elllo.org** (levels 1–3)
- **ESL Lab** (easy level)
- **EnglishClass101** beginner lessons

## Spelling Practice

Common words you must spell correctly:
- Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday
- January, February, March, April, May, June, July, August, September, October, November, December
- Numbers, addresses, email addresses

## Quick Wins

1. Read questions BEFORE listening.
2. Predict the answer type (number/name/place).
3. Write the answer immediately; don't wait.
4. Use the 30-second breaks to read ahead.
5. Check spelling in the last 10 minutes.
