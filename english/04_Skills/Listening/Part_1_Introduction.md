# Listening Part 1 — Introduction

## What to Expect

- A conversation between two people in an everyday context.
- Examples: booking a room, joining a gym, asking for information.
- 10 questions, usually form or note completion.

## Common Answers

- Names, phone numbers, dates, prices, addresses
- Times, types of service, numbers

## Tips

1. Read the form before listening.
2. Predict if the answer is a number, name, or word.
3. Listen for spelling of names and places.
4. Write exactly what you hear.
