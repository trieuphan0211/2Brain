# Listening Part 4 — Academic Talk

## What to Expect

- One speaker gives an academic lecture.
- Examples: science, history, environmental topics.
- Question types: note completion, sentence completion, summary completion.

## Tips

1. Read the whole task first to understand the topic.
2. Pay attention to signpost words: "first," "however," "in conclusion."
3. Don't worry if you miss one answer — move on quickly.
