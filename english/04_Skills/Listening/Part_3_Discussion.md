# Listening Part 3 — Discussion

## What to Expect

- A conversation between 2–4 speakers in an academic context.
- Examples: students discussing a project, assignment, or presentation.
- Question types: multiple choice, matching, sentence completion.

## Tips

1. Identify the speakers and their opinions.
2. Listen for agreement/disagreement.
3. Multiple choice often tests attitude or purpose, not just facts.
