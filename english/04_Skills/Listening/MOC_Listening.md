# Map of Content — Listening

## Overview

IELTS Listening has 4 parts, 40 questions, 30 minutes.

| Part | Context | Question types |
|------|---------|----------------|
| Part 1 | Everyday conversation (2 speakers) | form completion, note completion |
| Part 2 | Monologue (social context) | matching, maps, multiple choice |
| Part 3 | Academic discussion (2–4 speakers) | multiple choice, matching |
| Part 4 | Academic lecture (1 speaker) | note completion, sentence completion |

## General Tips

- Read questions before listening.
- Predict the type of answer (number, name, place).
- Check spelling after each section.
- Use the 30-second pauses to read ahead.
