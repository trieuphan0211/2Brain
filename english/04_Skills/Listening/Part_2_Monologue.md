# Listening Part 2 — Monologue

## What to Expect

- One speaker talks about a social topic.
- Examples: tour guide, event announcement, local facilities.
- Question types: matching, maps, multiple choice.

## Tips

1. For maps: know directions (north, south, left, right, opposite, next to).
2. For matching: read all options before listening.
3. Listen for synonyms — the speaker may not use the exact words in the options.
