# Skimming and Scanning

## Skimming

Read quickly to get the **main idea**.

- Read the title and first/last sentence of each paragraph.
- Look at headings and topic sentences.
- Don't stop for unknown words.

## Scanning

Read quickly to find **specific information**.

- Look for names, numbers, dates, keywords.
- Move your eyes fast across the text.
- Use your finger or cursor to guide you.

## When to Use

| Task | Technique |
|------|-----------|
| Matching headings | Skim first |
| True/False/Not Given | Scan first |
| Fill in blanks | Scan first |
| Understanding structure | Skim first |
