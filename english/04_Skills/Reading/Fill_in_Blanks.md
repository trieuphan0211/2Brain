# Fill in the Blanks

## Types

1. **Summary completion** — fill gaps in a summary of the passage.
2. **Sentence completion** — complete sentences using words from the passage.
3. **Table/flow-chart completion** — fill a table or diagram.

## Strategy

1. Predict the type of word needed (noun, verb, adjective).
2. Find the matching location in the passage.
3. Copy the exact word(s) from the passage.
4. Check the word limit (e.g., NO MORE THAN TWO WORDS).

## Tips

- Spelling must be correct.
- Grammatical form must fit the sentence.
- Don't change the word from the passage.
