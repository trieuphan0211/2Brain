# Map of Content — Reading

## Overview

IELTS Academic Reading has 3 passages, 40 questions, 60 minutes.

| Passage | Difficulty | Length |
|---------|------------|--------|
| 1 | Easier | ~800 words |
| 2 | Medium | ~900 words |
| 3 | Hardest | ~1000 words |

## Question Types

- [[True_False_Not_Given]]
- [[Matching_Headings]]
- [[Fill_in_Blanks]]
- [[Skimming_Scanning]]

## Time Management

| Passage | Time |
|---------|------|
| Passage 1 | 15–18 minutes |
| Passage 2 | 18–20 minutes |
| Passage 3 | 22–25 minutes |
| Transfer/check | 2–5 minutes |

## General Tips

- Read the questions first.
- Don't try to understand every word.
- Look for keywords and synonyms.
- Manage your time strictly.
