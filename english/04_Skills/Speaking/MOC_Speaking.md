# Map of Content — Speaking

## Overview

IELTS Speaking has 3 parts, 11–14 minutes.

| Part | Time | Task |
|------|------|------|
| Part 1 | 4–5 min | Answer questions about yourself |
| Part 2 | 3–4 min | Speak for 1–2 minutes on a cue card |
| Part 3 | 4–5 min | Discuss abstract ideas related to Part 2 |
