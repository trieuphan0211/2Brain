# Speaking Part 1 — Introduction

## Common Topics

- Hometown
- Study / Work
- Family
- Accommodation
- Hobbies
- Daily routine
- Food
- Travel

## Tips

1. Give short, direct answers (2–3 sentences).
2. Don't memorize long speeches.
3. Use natural language.
4. Add one small detail or reason.

## Sample Questions and Answers

**Q: Where do you live?**
> I live in Ho Chi Minh City. It is a big city in the south of Vietnam. I have lived there since I was born.

**Q: Do you work or study?**
> I am currently a student. I study information technology at a university. I want to become a software engineer.
