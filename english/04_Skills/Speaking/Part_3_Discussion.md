# Speaking Part 3 — Discussion

## What to Expect

- Abstract questions related to Part 2 topic.
- The examiner wants to see your ability to discuss ideas.

## Tips

1. Give a direct answer first.
2. Explain your reason.
3. Give an example.
4. Use simple linkers: because, however, for example.

## Useful Phrases

- I think the main reason is ...
- In my opinion, ...
- On the one hand ... on the other hand ...
- For example, ...
- In conclusion, ...

## Sample Question

**Q: Do you think reading is important for young people?**
> Yes, I think reading is very important for young people. First, it helps them improve their vocabulary and writing skills. Second, reading can increase knowledge about the world. For example, if young people read newspapers, they can learn about current events. Therefore, schools should encourage students to read more.
