# Pronunciation Tips

## Common Vietnamese Speaker Issues

1. **Final consonants** — pronounce /t/, /d/, /k/, /p/ clearly.
2. **/θ/ and /ð/** — "think" /θɪŋk/, "this" /ðɪs/.
3. **/ɪ/ vs /iː/** — "ship" /ʃɪp/ vs "sheep" /ʃiːp/.
4. **Word stress** — stress the correct syllable.
5. **Sentence stress** — stress content words, reduce function words.

## Practice Sentences

1. I **think** this is the **best** idea.
2. She **works** in a **hospital**.
3. Can you **help** me with this **problem**?

## Recording Checklist

- [ ] I speak clearly.
- [ ] I pronounce final sounds.
- [ ] I use natural intonation.
- [ ] I don't speak too fast.
