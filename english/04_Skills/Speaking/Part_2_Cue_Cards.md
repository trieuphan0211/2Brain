# Speaking Part 2 — Cue Cards

## Structure (1 minute preparation + 2 minutes speaking)

Use this structure:

1. **Introduction** — say what you will talk about.
2. **Description** — answer the bullet points.
3. **Reason/Example** — explain why or give an example.
4. **Conclusion** — one closing sentence.

## Useful Phrases

- I would like to talk about ...
- The first thing I want to mention is ...
- Another reason is ...
- In conclusion, ...

## Sample Cue Card

**Describe a book you enjoyed reading.**
- what the book is
- what it is about
- when you read it
- and explain why you enjoyed it

**Model Answer:**
> I would like to talk about a book called "The Alchemist." It is a famous novel by Paulo Coelho. I read it two years ago when I was in high school.
>
> The book is about a young shepherd who travels to find his treasure. During his journey, he learns many important lessons about life. The story is simple but meaningful.
>
> I enjoyed it because it made me think about my own dreams. It also taught me that I should not give up easily. After reading it, I felt more motivated.
>
> In conclusion, "The Alchemist" is my favorite book because it has an inspiring message.
