This folder tracks your personal mistakes. Reviewing errors regularly is one of the fastest ways to improve.

## Most Common Errors at Band 3.0

2. Subject-verb agreement
3. Wrong tense choice
4. Preposition errors

## How to Log an Error

Use the [[Error_Log_Entry]] template. Each entry should include:
- The original mistake
- The correction
- Why it is wrong
- A practice sentence
