# Grammar Errors

## Common Patterns

| Error Type | Example | Correction |
|------------|---------|------------|
| Missing article | I am student. | I am **a** student. |
| Subject-verb agreement | She go to school. | She **goes** to school. |
| Wrong tense | I go to Hanoi yesterday. | I **went** to Hanoi yesterday. |
| Preposition | I live in Hanoi city. | I live **in** Hanoi. |
| Plural | I have two book. | I have two **books**. |
