# Pronunciation Errors

## Common Patterns

| Sound | Example | Problem |
|-------|---------|---------|
| /θ/ | think | Saying "tink" or "sink" |
| /ð/ | this | Saying "dis" or "zis" |
| Final /t/ | want | Dropping the final sound |
| /ɪ/ vs /iː/ | ship / sheep | Confusing short and long vowels |
