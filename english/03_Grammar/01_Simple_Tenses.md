# Simple Tenses

## Rule

Simple tenses describe habits, facts, completed actions, and experiences.

## Formulas

| Tense | Formula | Example |
|-------|---------|---------|
| Present simple | S + V1(s/es) | She **works** every day. |
| Past simple | S + V2 | He **went** to school yesterday. |
| Present perfect | S + have/has + V3 | I **have finished** my homework. |

## Common Mistakes at Band 3.0

1. ❌ She **go** to school. → ✅ She **goes** to school.
2. ❌ I **goed** to the park. → ✅ I **went** to the park.
3. ❌ I **have** a test yesterday. → ✅ I **had** a test yesterday.
4. ❌ He **have** lived here. → ✅ He **has** lived here.

## Practice Sentences

1. She works (work) in a hospital. (present simple)
2. They visited __________ (visit) their grandma last week. (past simple)
3. I have never been (never / be) to Japan. (present perfect)
4. He doesn't like (not / like) coffee. (present simple)
5. We finished __________ (finish) the project two days ago. (past simple)

## IELTS Context

- **Speaking Part 1:** "Where do you live?" → "I **live** in Hanoi."
- **Writing Task 2:** Use present simple for facts and opinions.
- **Writing Task 1:** Use past simple for dates in the past.
