# Reported Speech

## Rule

Reported speech tells what someone said without using their exact words.

## Formulas

| Direct | Reported |
|--------|----------|
| present simple → | past simple |
| present continuous → | past continuous |
| past simple → | past perfect |
| will → | would |
| can → | could |

## Examples

- Direct: "I **am** tired," she said. → Reported: She said (that) she **was** tired.
- Direct: "I **will** help you," he said. → Reported: He said (that) he **would** help me.

## Common Mistakes at Band 3.0

1. ❌ She said that she **is** tired. → ✅ She said that she **was** tired.
2. ❌ He told that he was busy. → ✅ He **said that** he was busy. / He **told me** he was busy.
3. ❌ They said me the news. → ✅ They **told** me the news.

## Practice Sentences

1. "I am busy." → She said __________.
2. "I will call you." → He said __________.
3. "We went to the park." → They said __________.
4. "I can swim." → She said __________.
5. "He is working." → She said __________.

## IELTS Context

- **Speaking Part 2/3:** Report what someone told you.
- **Writing Task 2:** Sometimes report research findings.
