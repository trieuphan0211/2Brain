# Articles

## Rule

- **a / an** = one, any, first mention
- **the** = specific, already mentioned, unique
- **no article** = general plurals, abstract concepts, names

## Formulas

| Article | Use | Example |
|---------|-----|---------|
| a | before consonant sound | a book, a university |
| an | before vowel sound | an apple, an hour |
| the | specific | the book on the table |
| no article | general plurals | I like books. |

## Common Mistakes at Band 3.0

1. ❌ I am **a** student. (This is actually correct!) → Remember: **the** student = specific student.
2. ❌ She lives in **the** Hanoi. → ✅ She lives in **Hanoi**.
3. ❌ I like **the** music. → ✅ I like **music**.
4. ❌ He is **a** engineer. → ✅ He is **an** engineer.

## Practice Sentences

1. I have ___ dog. (a)
2. ___ moon is bright tonight. (the)
3. She is ___ honest person. (an)
4. ___ students in my class are friendly. (the)
5. I enjoy ___ reading. (no article)

## IELTS Context

- **Writing Task 2:** "**The** government should..." (specific government) vs "Governments should..." (general)
- **Speaking:** "I go to **the** gym."
