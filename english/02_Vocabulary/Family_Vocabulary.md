# Family Vocabulary

20 common words and phrases about family. Useful for IELTS Speaking Part 1 and Writing Task 2.

## People in the Family

| Word | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **nuclear family** | gia đình gồm bố, mẹ và con | I live in a *nuclear family* with my parents and one brother. |
| **extended family** | gia đình nội ngoại rộng | My *extended family* often meets at Tet. |
| **siblings** | anh chị em ruột | I have two *siblings*: an older sister and a younger brother. |
| **spouse** | vợ / chồng | Her *spouse* works in a hospital. |
| **ancestor** | tổ tiên | My *ancestors* came from a small village. |
| **descendant** | con cháu | We are *descendants* of farmers. |

## Family Relationships

| Word | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **close-knit** | gắn bó, khăng khít | My family is very *close-knit*. |
| **bond** | mối liên kết | The *bond* between parents and children is strong. |
| **generation gap** | khoảng cách thế hệ | The *generation gap* makes it hard to talk about social media. |
| **relative** | họ hàng | Most of my *relatives* live in the countryside. |
| **guardian** | ngườ giám hộ | My aunt became my legal *guardian*. |

## Family Roles / Duties

| Word | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **breadwinner** | trụ cột gia đình | My father is the *breadwinner* in our family. |
| **household chores** | việc nhà | Children should help with *household chores*. |
| **raise** | nuôi dưỡng | My parents worked hard to *raise* three children. |
| **upbringing** | sự dạy dỗ | I had a strict *upbringing*. |
| **responsibility** | trách nhiệm | Sharing *responsibilities* helps the family. |

## Family Types / Situations

| Word | Meaning | Example |
|------|---------|---------|
| **single-parent family** | gia đình đơn thân | Many children grow up in a *single-parent family*. |
| **blended family** | gia đình có con riêng | A *blended family* needs time to build trust. |
| **only child** | con một | As an *only child*, I sometimes feel lonely. |
| **foster family** | gia đình nuôi dưỡng trẻ mồ côi | He grew up with a *foster family*. |
| **orphan** | trẻ mồ côi | The charity helps *orphans* find homes. |

## Useful Phrases

- **get on well with** — hòa hợp với
  - I *get on well with* my older sister.
- **take after** — giống (tính cách / ngoại hình)
  - I *take after* my mother.
- **look up to** — tôn trọng, ngưỡng mộ
  - I *look up to* my grandfather.
- **fall out with** — cãi nhau, xích mích
  - I sometimes *fall out with* my brother.
