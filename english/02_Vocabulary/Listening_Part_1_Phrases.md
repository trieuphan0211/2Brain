# Listening Part 1 Phrases

Useful phrases and words from Listening Part 1 practice. Common in surveys, forms, and everyday conversations.

## Phrases

| Phrase                  | Meaning                                     | Example                                                    |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| **on behalf of**        | thay mặt cho                                | *On behalf of* the local council, thank you for your time. |
| **local council**       | hội đồng địa phương, chính quyền địa phương | The *local council* is planning new transport routes.      |
| **transport**           | phương tiện giao thông / vận tải            | Public *transport* in my city is cheap but crowded.        |
| **start by asking you** | bắt đầu bằng việc hỏi bạn                   | I'll *start by asking you* a few questions about your job. |
| **spare a few minutes** | dành vài phút                               | Could you *spare a few minutes* to answer some questions?  |

## Common Collocations

| Collocation | Meaning |
|-------------|---------|
| public transport | phương tiện giao thông công cộng |
| local council survey | khảo sát của chính quyền địa phương |
| spare some time | dành một ít thờ gian |
| on behalf of someone | thay mặt ai đó |

## Listening Tip

In Part 1, listen for these signal phrases. They often come **before** the answer you need to write down.

- "I'll *start by asking you*..." → the first question is coming.
- "*On behalf of*..." → tells you who is doing the survey.
- "Could you *spare a few minutes*?" → the speaker is asking for help.
