# Band 4.0 Target Vocabulary

Words to master on the way from 3.0 to 5.0.

| Word | Type | Definition | Example |
|------|------|------------|---------|
| advantage | noun | lợi thế | One advantage is low cost. |
| advertise | verb | quảng cáo | They advertise on social media. |
| affect | verb | ảnh hưởng | Smoking affects your health. |
| ambition | noun | tham vọng | His ambition is to become a doctor. |
| ancient | adj | cổ đại | We visited ancient temples. |
| announce | verb | thông báo | They announced the result yesterday. |
| anxiety | noun | lo âu | She felt anxiety before the exam. |
| apparent | adj | rõ ràng | It was apparent that he was tired. |
| approach | noun/verb | cách tiếp cận | We need a new approach. |
| appropriate | adj | phù hợp | Please wear appropriate clothes. |
| approximately | adv | xấp xỉ | The trip takes approximately two hours. |
| argue | verb | tranh luận | They often argue about money. |
| arrange | verb | sắp xếp | I will arrange the meeting. |
| assess | verb | đánh giá | We need to assess the damage. |
| assist | verb | hỗ trợ | Can you assist me with this? |
| assume | verb | giả định | I assume you are busy. |
| attempt | noun/verb | cố gắng | This is my first attempt. |
| attract | verb | thu hút | The city attracts many tourists. |
| available | adj | có sẵn | Tickets are still available. |
| aware | adj | nhận thức | I was not aware of the problem. |
