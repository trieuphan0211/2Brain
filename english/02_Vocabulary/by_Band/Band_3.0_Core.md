# Band 3.0 Core Vocabulary

Words you should already know or learn very quickly. These are essential for forming basic sentences.

| Word | Type | Definition | Example |
|------|------|------------|---------|
| ability | noun | khả năng | She has the ability to learn fast. |
| able | adj | có thể | I am able to help you. |
| about | prep | về | We talked about the exam. |
| above | prep | ở trên | The picture is above the desk. |
| accept | verb | chấp nhận | I accept your offer. |
| account | noun | tài khoản | I have a bank account. |
| across | prep | qua | Walk across the street. |
| action | noun | hành động | We need to take action. |
| activity | noun | hoạt động | Outdoor activity is good for health. |
| actually | adv | thực ra | Actually, I don't agree. |
| add | verb | thêm | Add salt to the soup. |
| adult | noun | ngườii lớn | This film is for adults only. |
| advice | noun | lờii khuyên | Can you give me some advice? |
| affect | verb | ảnh hưởng | The weather affects my mood. |
| afraid | adj | sợ | I am afraid of dogs. |
| after | prep/conj | sau | After class, I went home. |
| afternoon | noun | buổi chiều | Good afternoon! |
| again | adv | lại | Please say that again. |
| against | prep | chống lại | We played against another team. |
