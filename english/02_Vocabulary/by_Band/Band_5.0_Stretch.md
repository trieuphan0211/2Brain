# Band 5.0 Stretch Vocabulary

Words that will push you toward Band 5.0. Learn these after you are comfortable with Band 4.0 words.

| Word | Type | Definition | Example |
|------|------|------------|---------|
| acknowledge | verb | công nhận | He acknowledged his mistake. |
| acquire | verb | đạt được | It takes time to acquire a new skill. |
| adapt | verb | thích nghi | We need to adapt to changes. |
| adequate | adj | đầy đủ | The food was adequate for everyone. |
| advocate | verb/noun | ủng hộ / ngườii ủng hộ | She advocates for better education. |
| allocate | verb | phân bổ | The government allocated funds for schools. |
| anticipate | verb | dự kiến | We anticipate some problems. |
| compensate | verb | đền bù | The company will compensate customers. |
| component | noun | thành phần | Each component is important. |
| comprehensive | adj | toàn diện | We need a comprehensive plan. |
| concentrate | verb | tập trung | I need to concentrate on my study. |
| conclude | verb | kết luận | The report concludes that more research is needed. |
| consequence | noun | hậu quả | The consequence was very serious. |
| considerable | adj | đáng kể | There is considerable support for the idea. |
| contribute | verb | đóng góp | Exercise contributes to good health. |
| conventional | adj | truyền thống | Conventional methods may not work. |
| demonstrate | verb | chứng minh | The study demonstrates the benefits. |
| discourage | verb | làm nản lòng | High prices discourage buyers. |
| emphasize | verb | nhấn mạnh | The report emphasizes the need for change. |
